Key takeaway for practitioners: AI systems produce citations with the same confident tone regardless of whether those citations exist. There is no "I'm not sure" signal. This is not a bug that will be fixed — it is a structural property of how language models generate text. Any legal workflow relying on AI-generated research must include independent citation verification.
